Our Expertise

At Lovely Developments, we specialise in helping drinks brands perfect their products. Whether you are a start-up or an established brand exploring new markets, we can turn a good idea into a great drink.

Our Services

  • Recipe tweaking to perfect or simplify your products
  • Creating commercial, scalable recipes
  • Sourcing ingredients from accredited and trusted suppliers that enable you to scale up
  • Short test-run canning and bottling at our SALSA-accredited facility near Bristol
  • Using our award-winning tonics and botanical mixers to create your G&T in a can or other RTDs
  • Ensuring compliance with current food and drink regulations
  • Providing technical data sheets for your products
  • Microbiology reports and accelerated shelf-life testing from a UKAS-accredited lab
